I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the David R. Cheriton School of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo, advisored by Professor [Jian Zhao](https://www.jeffjianzhao.com/).My research lies at the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I aim to understand and develop novel AI models and design interactive tools to achieve Human-AI collaboration. I also conduct in-depth user studies to understand the pitfalls when human interacting with AI models. 

I received my B.Eng. in Computer Engineer from Jilin University and M.Sc. degree in Information Technology from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.
